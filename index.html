Navigating Freely

With this program, you have all videos and songs on Youtube in one place. All channels or videos or playlists or search links, you add, and all recorded songs, you add, are easy to access without you need to connect on Youtube. You can add every of that on 2 pages. When you positioning your mouse cursor on bottom of the program, a menu barre is appearing. Here, there are several buttons... For close the program, for minimize the program, for go backward, for go forward, for open the page of links, for reload the page, for go on a link, then there is a box for put the link to go, and also the buttons, for record audio, for open a tuner audio, for open the page of songs, for clear cache of the browser, and finally the current time of the day. The principle is to skip video durating ads and to not be invaded by annoying suggestions following your history of browsing Youtube. Have easy access to worldwide Youtube videos without Youtube annoying features tracking you everytime you go to watch videos. Don't be annoyed to always have to skip ads for watch the next suggested video. Have fun listening all your music and recording songs with a simple button and low size files. Find easy your music and your Youtube channels, videos, playlists and search links with a search tool with autocompletion and coloring in orange the text of buttons for play songs or for going to links.

Have fun to watch Youtube or listen your songs. Have fun with easy access and easy to edit your custom pages for linking channels or songs you have on your computer. Considerations About the Program

The program is only in fullscreen window. After recorded a song, the directory of corresponding file is automatically open. You can uninstall the tuner audio, simply the button to open it, will be disable. Despite this program, I made a Edge extension to cut ads on Youtube named noads-extension. The extension can be installed from edge://extensions/. The extension must be open each time you load a new tab or reload a page. It skipping almost all ads in times, ads are far less invasive. I also made another Edge extension named tiktok-voicer-extension. It's for control with voice speech TikTok or Youtube Shorts using next, previous, like, unlike, play, in microphone, enabling browsing short videos. Also another Edge extension I made named speech-comments-extension. It reading comments under any Youtube video when you open it, but you must display some commentaries by scrolling down the Youtube video page, before open it. My source codes and program are here.
